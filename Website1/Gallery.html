The Dangers of Ocean Pollution:

1. The Plastic Pandemic

  • Every year, an estimated 8 million tons of plastic end up in our oceans.
  • There are approximately 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic debris in the ocean.

2. Impact on Marine Life:

  • Over 100,000 marine mammals and one million seabirds are killed by plastic pollution annually.
  • Plastic debris has been found in the deepest parts of the ocean, affecting even the most remote ecosystems.

3. Fisheries Threat:

  • Plastic pollution disrupts and contaminates fish habitats, affecting global fisheries.
  • It is estimated that over 200 species of marine life are at risk due to the ingestion of plastic.
